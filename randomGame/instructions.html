Instructions

Welcome to the Random Game! Here are the instructions:

  1. Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
  2. Once the game starts, a random number or item will be displayed.
  3. Your task is to guess the next random number or item.
  4. Enter your guess in the provided input field.
  5. Click the "Submit" button to submit your guess.
  6. If your guess is correct, you win!
  7. If your guess is incorrect, you can try again.
